Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

Q.

How long does a brow lamination treatment take ?

A.

The brow lamination process can take up to 12 minutes, however, other tinting and hair removal techniques are incorporated into the treatment, it should take 45 minutes.

Q.

How long does brow lamination last?

A.

Brow lamination results last up to 6-12 weeks following treatment.

Q.

Is brow lamination safe?

A.

Yes, brow lamination is a perfectly safe treatment. As with any eyebrow treatment, your artist will ask relevant questions and undertake the relevant patch policy to ensure that the treatment and products are suitable for you.

Q.

Eyebrow lamination aftercare advice

A.

It’s really easy to care for your brows following a lamination treatment. For the first 24 hours, you should treat your brows with care, avoiding getting them wet, steam baths and saunas. We also recommend that you avoid pressure, especially sleeping on them for the first 24-48 hrs. After this time, you can treat them normal, simply brushing into place every morning with a spoolie.

Q.

Is brow lamination the same as a lash lift?

A.

Brow lamination is not exactly the same as a lash lift, but uses the same basic principles.

Q.

Is brow lamination permanent?

A.

Brow lamination is not permanent; results usually last up to 6-12 weeks.
